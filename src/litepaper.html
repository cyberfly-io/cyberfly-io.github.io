What is Cyberfly?

Cyberfly is a web 3.0 scalable decentralized internet of things platform.

Connecting

Connect your device with blockchain

  • Manage your devices
  • Store data in blockchain

Monitoring

Monitor your device and device data

  • Monitor your device's sensor data with cyberfly platform's dashboard
  • Monitor the availability, and health of your IoT devices

Controlling

Control your device equipments with cyberfly platform

  • Directly send the commands to device
  • Write your rules and execute the action based the rules

Tokenomics

Cyberfly platform users need to hold some specified amount of CFLY tokens to use cyberfly IoT platform.
