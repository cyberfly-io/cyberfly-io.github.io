TOKEN & UTILITY

CFLY Token & Utilities

At the core of our platform is CFLY, the utility token that powers the entire Cyberfly ecosystem. CFLY is crucial for device registration and sustaining our operations through a unique burn and treasury model.

Device Registration

Users utilize CFLY tokens to register new devices on the platform.

Token Burning

A portion of every transaction fee is permanently burned, creating a deflationary supply.
